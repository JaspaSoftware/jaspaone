# Project Profile: Jaspa One

**Version:** 1.0  
**Domain:** Business Automation, HR, CRM, and Project Management  
**Mission:** Streamline and automate business operations to empower data-driven decision making.

---

## 🏗️ Platform Overview

Jaspa One is a robust, all-in-one business management platform designed to unify essential operational tools—HR, CRM, and Project Management—into a single, cohesive interface. It eliminates fragmented workflows by providing a centralized source of truth for all business data.

## 🌟 Core Value Proposition

- **Operational Excellence:** Automate routine tasks like attendance, invoicing, and contract management.
- **Enhanced Collaboration:** Real-time communication via internal chat and external integrations (Slack, Pusher).
- **Data-Driven Insights:** Advanced reporting systems for financial, project, and team performance metrics.
- **Scalability:** Modular architecture allows for seamless integration of Purchase, Stock, and Affiliate management tools.

## 🛠️ Key Capabilities

| Pillar | Key Features |
| :--- | :--- |
| **CRM** | Lead tracking, Client lifecycle management, Proposals, and Estimates. |
| **HRM** | Attendance, Leave management, Roster automation, and Employee Appreciations. |
| **Projects** | Kanban boards, Gantt charts, Milestone tracking, and Miro integration. |
| **Finance** | Invoicing, Credit notes, Multi-gateway payments, and Tax management. |
| **Support** | Multi-channel Ticket management and Client Portal access. |

## 🚀 Strategic Roadmap

Jaspa One is continuously evolving, with recent and upcoming focuses on:
- **Automation:** AI-driven shift rosters and automated lead follow-ups.
- **Integration:** Deepening connections with third-party tools like QuickBooks and Miro.
- **Accessibility:** Continued UI/UX refinement for mobile and multi-platform access.
- **Expansion:** Launching specialized modules for Inventory, QR Code management, and Affiliate networks.
